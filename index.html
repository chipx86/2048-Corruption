Join the numbers and get to the 2048 tile!

Beware the corruption tiles. Clean out any corrupted tiles, or you'll never hit 2048!

Keep going Try again

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Beware +1 and -1 tiles. They can match with and corrupt any tile. You'll want to cancel them out or combine them into useful blank "eraser" tiles.

Eraser tiles can also appear at any time. They might hurt or help you. Use them to get rid of the corruption, but make sure you don't erase your good tiles!

You will also lose points for any corruption matches you make, unless you can convert them nicely back into a power of 2.

Note: This is not the official version of 2048.
